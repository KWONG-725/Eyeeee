How to Play

Objective

Look at the targets on the screen and keep your eyes focused on them to score points. The longer you maintain eye contact, the higher your score!

How to Play

  1. Allow camera access when prompted
  2. Click "Calibrate" to calibrate your eye tracking (optional but recommended)
  3. Click "Start Game" to begin
  4. Look at the targets that appear on screen
  5. Keep your eyes focused on a target to select it
  6. When the progress ring fills up, the target is selected and you score points!

Settings

  • Webcam Settings: Adjust camera selection, mirror options, brightness, and contrast
  • Game Settings: Choose difficulty, duration, target size, and audio options
  • High Contrast Mode: Enable for better visibility

Difficulty Levels

  • Easy: Large targets, slow movement, more time to select
  • Medium: Medium targets, moderate movement, balanced timing
  • Hard: Small targets, fast movement, quick selection required

Tips for Best Experience

  • Ensure good lighting for better eye detection
  • Keep your face centered in the webcam view
  • Sit at a comfortable distance from the screen
  • Use calibration for better accuracy
  • Take breaks if you experience eye strain
